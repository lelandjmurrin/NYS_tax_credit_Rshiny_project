New York State Article 9A Tax Credit Prediction Analysis

Purpose:

The purpose of this application is to assist tax professional consultants with the best tools for advising their clients.
This will help the customers make the most appropriate current and future business decisions when considering possible tax credits.

This tool allows users to select from two different datasets provided by the New York State Government: Corporate Tax Credit by Size of Entire Net Income and Corporate Tax Credits by Major Industry Group. Selecting a specific Income/Industry Group, Credit Name and Tax Year will generate a projection of the range of possible Average Tax Credit Earned per Taxpayer in dollars.

Steps:

  1. On the sidebar, select the desired dataset (Income or Industry)
  2. Select the appropriate Income or Industry Group
  3. Select the Credit Name (the type of credit awarded)
  4. Select the Tax Year for the prediction
  5. Finally, click on the Forecast tab above to see the results

Special Notes:

  • The plot produced shows the confidence interval of each prediction based on a range of values for the possible
    Number of Taxpayers (the number of corporations that earned this specific credit)
    for the selected Income or Industry group, Credit Name and Tax Year
  • 2019 is the last year in which the data was reported and predictions for this year are included in this analysis for comparison purposes
