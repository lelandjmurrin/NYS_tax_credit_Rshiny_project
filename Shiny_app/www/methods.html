Data Transformation Steps

These are the steps that were used to transform the raw data to a cleaned version that was used for the regression step in this model.

Steps:

  1. Filtered 'Credit Type' field to only consider 'Credit Earned'
  2. Split the dataset into known Totals for each 'Credit Name' and other records
  3. Joined the two tables on 'Tax Year' and 'Credit Name'
  4. Filtered out any missing Total Values that prevent imputation
  5. For the missing values, calculated the average 'Number of Taxpayers' and average 'Amount of Credit'
  6. Imputed the average values into the missing values
  7. Calculated the average amount of credit earned per taxpayer ('Avg') and being careful not to divide by 0
  8. The following columns were selected: 'Tax Year', 'Credit Name', 'Income/Industry Group', 'Number of Taxpayers', and 'Average Amount of Credit per Taxpayer'
